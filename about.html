About Us

Reel Video Resizer is a free online tool that helps users convert any video into a perfect 9:16 vertical format for social media platforms.

Our tool provides multiple modes including Fit, Fill, Space, and Blur to ensure your video looks professional and optimized for reels and shorts.

Built with modern web technologies, our mission is to provide a fast, privacy-friendly, and easy-to-use browser-based video editing experience.
